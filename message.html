A Message from Our Leaders


From our Don, Consigliere and many more...

Dear Reader,

Cam Lo family traces its roots to a small neighborhood in Cam Lo hamlet, Hung Vuong Ward, Hong Bang District, Hai Phong City, Vietnam (now Cam Lo, Hung Vuong Ward, Hai Phong City, Vietnam). It was here, in this humble and modest beginning, that the founding vision of Cam Lo Group first took shape: a dream and a determination to contribute meaningfully to the growth and development of Vietnam.

Guided by this vision, Cam Lo Group has grown into a diversified conglomerate spanning multiple industries. Our ambition is not merely to expand, but to bring lasting value and exceptional experiences to every customer we serve, upholding the same spirit of dedication that defined our earliest days.

Each subsidiary within the Cam Lo ecosystem operates with its own expertise and identity, while sharing a common commitment to quality, integrity, and long term value creation. For any questions regarding a specific product or service, we invite you to reach out directly to the relevant subsidiary. For matters concerning Cam Lo Group as a whole, please contact us through the email address provided on our homepage.

We thank you for visiting, and we look forward to growing together with you on this journey.

Sincerely,

The Leadership Team, Cam Lo Group.
